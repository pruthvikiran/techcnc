About Us

ABOUT TECH CNC SOLUTIONS

Tech CNC Solutions was stated in the year of 2012 as the known service provider and traders of the wide range of WIRE EDM and SINKER EDM Machines, and supplier of wedm spare parts and consumables. We are delighted to have a team of highly dedicated and skilled team of professionals, who possess excellent industrial knowledge and experience.

Tech CNC Solutions offers wide range of used machinery to our customer and supply edm consumables & wear parts for all wedm machines like Electrode cable, Carbide, Wire guide, Filters, Nozzles, Deion resin, work piece fixtures, Rust & Carbon Remover, we supply all material with good quality, customer satisfaction is our top priority.

Wide range of services

Tech CNC Solutions is independent service provider for AGIE CHARMILLES WEDM & EDM machines, over the years we have built our reputation on a professional, reliable and fast service that’s tailored to each customer’s needs. We provide the repair service Support around AGIECHARMILLES EDM & Wire EDM Machines.

  • Trouble shooting of Electronics and Mechanical problems
  • preventive maintenance/periodical maintenance
  • Component level PCBs Repair
  • Stock maintained for Spare PCBs
  • Operation, Programming & Maintenance Training for new operators
  • Machine Installation & Commissioning
  • AMC Services
